Animations

Powtoon

Powtoon is an animation tool that allows users to create engaging videos and presentations. It offers a variety of templates and a massive media library, making it easy for educators, students, and administrative staff to create dynamic learning materials, assignments, and announcements.

Educators can use Powtoon to create animated presentations that make content more engaging for students. For example, teachers can develop lessons that explain complex concepts through animations, making them easier to understand. Additionally, students can use Powtoon to create their own presentations, which can serve as a form of assessment and help them demonstrate their understanding of the material.

Go to the tool Price: Student ($4/month), Teacher ($8/month) Difficulty: Moderate

Screen Recording Tools

Camtasia

Camtasia is a powerful and user-friendly tool that offers a range of features for creating professional-quality videos. It allows users to record their screen, camera, microphone, and system audio with exceptional clarity, making it ideal for capturing lectures, tutorials, and presentations. The editor's drag-and-drop functionality simplifies video production, enabling educators to import various media, apply filters, transitions, and animations, and enhance their videos with callouts, shapes, and motion backgrounds.

Educators can use Camtasia to create engaging course content by recording lectures, demonstrating software, or providing visual explanations of complex concepts. The tool's ability to add captions, subtitles, and voiceovers ensures accessibility and clarity for all students. Additionally, Camtasia's AI features, such as background removal and lifelike voice narration, streamline the video creation process, allowing educators to focus on delivering high-quality educational material. Camtasia offers significant benefits by enhancing the learning experience. It supports diverse learning styles through visual and auditory content, increases student engagement with interactive elements, and provides a flexible platform for creating and sharing instructional videos. This versatility helps educators deliver content more effectively, cater to different learning needs, and improve overall student comprehension and retention.

Go to the tool Price: US$179,88 / year Difficulty: Moderate
Free Screen Recorder by ScreenPal

ScreenPal has a free screen recording tool that allows users to capture any area of their screen, including microphone audio and webcam video. It offers features like webcam background removal, drawing tools, and an integrated video editor for creating professional-quality videos. Users can upload unlimited recordings to a free hosting account for easy management and sharing.

Educators can use this tool to create engaging tutorials, lectures, and demo videos. They can annotate screencasts, add captions for accessibility, and record virtual events or live webinars. This makes it easier to provide personalized feedback and create interactive learning materials. It benefits educators by enhancing their ability to deliver content in a dynamic and interactive way. It supports diverse learning styles, increases student engagement, and provides a flexible platform for remote and hybrid learning.

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Easy
iTop Screen Recorder

iTop Screen Recorder is an intuitive screen recording tool for Windows, designed to capture every activity on your screen. It offers multiple recording modes, including full screen, customized area, and specific window recording. With features like webcam and audio recording, drawing and annotation tools, and a real-time AI voice changer, it is ideal for creating engaging educational content.

Educators can use iTop Screen Recorder to create lecture and presentation videos, record online courses, and capture live streams for later review. The teleprompter feature helps educators deliver their content smoothly, while the annotation tools allow them to highlight key points and add text comments during recordings. This makes it easier for students to follow along and understand complex concepts. iTop Screen Recorder allows educators to create high-quality, interactive videos. It also supports flipped classroom models, where students can watch recorded lectures at their own pace and come to class prepared for discussions. 

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Easy/Moderate/Difficult
Loom

Loom is a versatile edtech tool that allows educators to easily create and share video recordings. With features like screen and cam recording, drawing tools, and customizable recording dimensions, educators can use Loom to enhance their curriculum by adding context to assignments, personalizing learning experiences, and providing clear instructions. Videos can be shared via email, links, or messaging apps, making it easy for students to access and engage with the content.
Loom benefits both instructors and students by enabling flexible learning. Students can watch videos at their own pace, pause, and rewind as needed, which helps them better understand complex topics. Loom also has the ability to track student engagement, which fosters a more interactive and supportive learning environment.

Go to the tool Price: Free starter plan Difficulty: Easy
WeVideo

WeVideo's screen recorder allows users to record their screen, webcam, or both directly from their internet browser without needing any software or app. Educators can use this tool to create engaging tutorials, record lectures, and provide personalized feedback to students. By capturing their desktop and adding webcam footage, teachers can create a more interactive and personal learning experience.

In higher education, this tool benefits both instructors and students by enabling asynchronous learning. Educators can record lectures and make them available for students to watch at their convenience, facilitating better understanding and retention of material. Additionally, it helps in reducing the need for long meetings by allowing educators to send quick video updates and feedback, thus streamlining communication and enhancing the overall learning experience.

Video Creation & Editing

Clipchamp

Clipchamp is a video editing tool that is accessible directly from your browser, making it an excellent edtech resource. It allows users to create professional-quality videos without needing advanced technical skills or expensive software. With features like recording, editing, and captioning all in one app, Clipchamp is user-friendly and efficient.

Educators can enhance their courses by creating engaging video content for lectures, tutorials, and presentations. They can also encourage students to use the tool for assignments, projects, and presentations, fostering creativity and digital literacy.

It is particularly useful in education for crafting engaging and interactive educational videos. The platform's user-friendly interface ensures that even those with no prior video editing experience can produce high-quality content.
