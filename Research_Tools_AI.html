AI Research Tools

Unlock advanced research capabilities with AI-powered tools that support students and researchers in finding, analyzing, and organizing academic resources. These tools streamline the research process by helping you work smarter and more efficiently on any academic project.

Connected Papers

Connected Papers is a unique, visual tool designed to assist researchers and applied scientists in finding and exploring academic papers relevant to their field of work.

The tool generates a graph that visually represents the connections between the origin paper and other related papers.
The papers in the graph are arranged based on their similarity. Similar papers cluster together, while less related ones are pushed apart. Researchers gain a real, visual understanding of trends, popular works, and field dynamics. Connected Papers streamlines literature search, enhances accessibility, and provides valuable insights for researchers across diverse scientific domains. 

Go to the tool Price: Free option of 5 graphs p/m Difficulty: Easy
Elicit

Elicit is an AI research assistant that empowers researchers to analyze research papers at superhuman speed using natural language and machine learning.

It generates concise summaries of research articles and researchers can extract relevant information from papers into organized tables.

In summary, Elicit accelerates research workflows, enhances accuracy, and revolutionizes evidence-based decision-making. 

Go to the tool Price: Limited free option Difficulty: Easy
Globe Explorer

Globe Explorer is an AI-powered visual search engine designed to facilitate learning and research through visuals and images. It creates a detailed table of contents on any chosen topic, accompanied by optimal images or diagrams, making complex subjects more accessible and engaging. The tool also provides AI-generated overviews for each segment, enhancing the learning experience.

Educators and students can use Globe Explorer to explore new topics visually, making it easier to understand and retain information. Educators can integrate it into their teaching methods to provide dynamic and interactive lessons, while students can use it for research and study, benefiting from the visual aids and structured content.

Globe Explorer can enhance learning by presenting information in a visually appealing and interactive format. This approach not only increases student engagement but also aids in better comprehension and retention of complex subjects. The tool's ability to simplify research and learning processes makes it a valuable asset for both educators and students. 

Go to the tool Price: Limited free mode Difficulty: Easy
Litmaps

Litmaps is an innovative tool designed to streamline the literature review process for researchers, students, and professionals. It leverages citation networks to discover relevant academic papers, visualize research connections, and provide automated updates on new publications. This tool is particularly beneficial for educators as it allows them to integrate comprehensive literature reviews into their courses, helping students quickly find key papers and understand the broader context of their research topics.

Educators can use Litmaps to enhance their teaching by creating "seed maps" of essential readings, facilitating collaborative research projects, and keeping course materials up-to-date with the latest research. This not only aids in teaching critical thinking and research skills but also ensures that students are engaging with the most current and relevant literature.

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Easy
Paperguide.ai

Paperguide is an all-in-one AI-powered research platform designed to assist with discovering, reading, writing, and managing research. It offers features like AI Research Assistant, Reference Manager, and Writing Assistant, which help users understand papers, manage references, annotate/take notes, and enhance their writing.

By using the AI Research Assistant, students can quickly grasp complex topics and synthesize information from multiple sources. The Reference Manager allows for efficient organization of references, making it easier for students to cite sources accurately. Additionally, the AI Writing Assistant helps students improve their writing skills by providing intelligent paraphrasing, grammar checking, and contextual in-text citations.

Paperguide saves time and enhances the quality of research and writing. It enables students to focus more on critical thinking and analysis rather than the tedious aspects of research management. This tool also promotes academic integrity with its built-in plagiarism checker, ensuring that students' work is original and properly cited.

Go to the tool Price: Limited free tier Difficulty: Easy
Semantic Scholar

Semantic Scholar is a free, AI-powered research tool for scientific literature. It aims to make scientific research more accessible and richly contextual through features like the Semantic Reader, which enhances the reading experience by providing deeper insights and connections within the text.

Educators can integrate Semantic Scholar into their courses by using it to source high-quality, peer-reviewed papers for reading assignments, literature reviews, and research projects. The platform's advanced search capabilities allow educators to find relevant papers quickly, making it easier to curate up-to-date and comprehensive reading lists for their students.

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Easy
Scinapse

Scinapse is a tool designed to help users identify major industry challenges, find top countries, research institutions, and researchers by ranking, and easily access a list of peer reviewers. It also provides field trends, graphs, and much more information.

Educators can use Scinapse in their courses to enhance research capabilities by guiding students to relevant literature, identifying key researchers and institutions in their field, and staying updated with the latest trends and challenges. This can be particularly useful for assignments, research projects, and staying current with industry developments.

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Easy
SciSpace

SciSpace is an all-in-one AI-powered tool designed to assist students and researchers in understanding and analyzing academic texts. It offers features like summarizing research papers, expanding notes into paragraphs, discovering new papers, and citing sources in various formats.

Educators can use SciSpace to enhance their courses by providing students with tools to better comprehend complex research materials, generate research-backed explanations, and improve the fluency of their content. This tool can also help in creating interactive videos about research papers, making learning more engaging.

Go to the tool Price: Limited free tier Difficulty: Easy
Skolar AI

Skolar AI is an innovative platform designed to enhance research and writing skills for students, researchers, and academics.

Skolar AI empowers users to obtain fast, relevant results by asking any research question. Users can interact with research papers directly, making the reading experience more insightful. Skolar AI provides comprehensive responses with thorough research and proper citations.

Skolar AI transforms research and writing by providing instant access to comprehensive information, making it an invaluable tool for educators and students in higher education.

Unriddle

Unriddle is an advanced AI-powered tool designed to enhance research and writing processes. It allows users to quickly extract and summarize data from documents, highlight text to find relevant sources, and map work into interactive graphs to uncover hidden patterns. Unriddle also offers features like autocompletion, writing suggestions, and improvements, making it a comprehensive tool for managing and understanding complex information.

By using Unriddle, students can efficiently navigate through extensive readings, annotate and summarize key points, and collaborate on projects with a single source of truth. This tool can be particularly useful for assignments that require in-depth literature reviews or data analysis.
