About this site

EdTech Tools is your go-to repository for discovering, exploring, and applying a wide variety of educational technology tools designed to enhance learning and teaching within higher education. From innovative apps to powerful online platforms, our collection provides educators, students, and instructional designers with practical, up-to-date information about tools that support both learning and teaching processes. Whether you're looking for AI-driven applications, graphic resources, or programming resources, EdTech Tools is here to help you find the technology you need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between educators, students, and technology by providing a well-organized, user-friendly repository that covers not only essential edtech tools but also offers helpful resources for integrating them into academic environments. We aim to simplify technology adoption by offering clear guidance on how these tools can be effectively used and maintained.

What You’ll Find In This Site:
  1. Educational Technology Tools

    Each tool in our repository comes with:
    • A concise summary of the tool’s purpose and capabilities.
    • Practical applications for both educators and students within higher education.
    • Highlights of how the tool can enhance teaching, learning, and productivity.
  2. Resource Categories

    Alongside edtech tools, we provide additional resources that support academic needs and skill development, including:

    Graphic Resources:     Downloadable images, fonts, templates, and design assets for classroom presentations, projects, and instructional content.

    AI Resources: Information on AI best practices, prompt creation guides, and academic articles to help educators and students use AI tools effectively.

    Programming/Coding Resources: Access to online courses, free tutorials, and comprehensive guides for learning programming skills that are increasingly valuable in today’s digital landscape.
