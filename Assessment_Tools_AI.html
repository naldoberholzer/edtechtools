Assessment Bot

Assessment Bot is designed to streamline the assessment process for educators. It allows teachers to use their regular assessments or copy and paste questions from paper-based assessments into Google Forms. The tool works with most regular exam mark schemes without modifications, and it will soon feature a mark scheme optimizer to maximize accuracy via AI.

Educators can use Assessment Bot by simply copying and pasting their existing mark schemes. The tool automatically handles data entry and provides personalized feedback documents for each student, detailing their performance, areas for improvement, and model answers. These feedback documents can be distributed via Google Classroom or email, making it versatile for different school ecosystems.

In the higher education environment, Assessment Bot offers significant benefits by reducing the manual workload of grading and providing detailed, personalized feedback to students. This allows educators to focus more on teaching and less on administrative tasks, ultimately enhancing the learning experience for students.

Bongo

Bongo is an edtech tool that enhances coaching effectiveness through AI and human-driven asynchronous feedback. It allows learners to receive personalized, objective feedback 24/7, enabling them to practice and refine their skills anytime, anywhere. This tool helps instructors provide more effective and contextualized feedback, fostering a collaborative learning environment and streamlining the assessment process with clear criteria for consistent evaluation.

Educators can use Bongo in their courses by incorporating structured video workflows that facilitate repeated skill practice and knowledge application within real-world contexts. They can also create activities where learners answer questions asynchronously with limited time or collaborate on group activities either synchronously or asynchronously. Additionally, Bongo keeps learners engaged through multiple-choice questions or video responses throughout a video. Bongo builds communication and critical thinking skills as learners continuously practice, self-assess, and deliver video presentations. It accelerates skill development, improves performance, and creates a more inclusive and diverse learning experience.

Knowt

Knowt is a free AI-powered study app designed to assist students with homework, lectures, and class work. It creates flashcards and practice questions from notes, videos, and slides using AI. Students can study with spaced repetition, flashcards, practice tests, and more. Knowt also offers pre-made study guides and resources for major exams, and allows importing flashcards from other services like Quizlet.

Educators can use Knowt in both physical classrooms and online settings. In a physical classroom, teachers can create and share flashcards and practice questions with students to reinforce learning. Online, educators can integrate Knowt into their virtual lessons, providing students with interactive study materials and practice tests that can be accessed anytime. This flexibility ensures that students have continuous access to study resources, regardless of their learning environment.

Knowt promotes active learning and self-assessment. The app's AI-generated content helps students focus on key concepts and improve retention through spaced repetition. Additionally, the ability to import and customize study materials makes it a versatile tool for diverse academic needs, enhancing the overall learning experience.

QuestionWell

QuestionWell is an AI-powered tool designed to help educators create interactive classroom activities, sub plans, and summative assessments. It offers various export options and allows users to include multiple choice, fill in the blanks, and short answer questions. The AI aligns questions with educational standards, defines technical vocabulary, and links to Wikipedia for further reading.

Educators can use QuestionWell in both physical and online classrooms. In a physical classroom, teachers can create and print activities or assessments, while in an online setting, students can complete questions by entering a code on the QuestionWell website.

QuestionWell provides tailored, standards-aligned questions, saving educators time, and ensuring that students engage with material in a meaningful way. This tool supports collaborative authoring and offers flexibility in how content is delivered and assessed.
