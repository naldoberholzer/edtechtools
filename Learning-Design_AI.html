5 Minute Lesson Plan

The 5 Minute Lesson Plan is a tool designed to streamline the lesson planning process for educators to quickly create focused lesson plans. By entering details like topic, subject, lesson length, and year group, the AI generates a comprehensive plan that can be edited and customized.

Educators can use this tool to efficiently plan their courses by filling out the provided template or letting the AI do the work. The generated plans can be easily saved, shared, and edited, making it convenient for teachers to prepare lessons on the go.

This tool offers significant benefits by saving time and reducing the workload associated with detailed lesson planning. It allows educators to focus more on teaching and engaging with students, enhancing the overall learning experience.

Brisk Teaching

Brisk is an edtech tool designed to streamline the creation of instructional materials by integrating seamlessly with platforms like Google Docs, Slides, and YouTube. It allows educators to quickly generate a variety of teaching resources, including slide decks, quizzes, lesson plans, and administrative documents, directly within their existing workflow.

Educators can use Brisk to transform engaging articles or YouTube videos into educational content instantly, eliminating the need for copy-pasting. This tool also supports the rapid differentiation, translation, and leveling of materials, making it easier to cater to diverse student needs. Additionally, Brisk simplifies administrative tasks by enabling the quick creation of emails, newsletters, and recommendation letters.

In the higher education environment, Brisk offers significant benefits by boosting teacher productivity and saving hours of work weekly. It ensures that educators can focus more on teaching and less on administrative tasks, ultimately enhancing the overall learning experience for students.

Diffit

Diffit is an edtech tool designed to help teachers create "just right" instructional materials, saving time and ensuring all students can access grade-level content.

Educators can use Diffit in their courses to tailor materials to the diverse needs of their students, ensuring that each student receives content appropriate for their learning level. This customization helps address different learning styles and abilities within a classroom, making the teaching process more effective and inclusive.

Diffit enables instructors to quickly generate and adapt course materials to meet the varied needs of their students. This flexibility enhances the learning experience and allows educators to focus more on teaching and less on material preparation, ultimately improving educational outcomes.

Persona Maker

(part of the The AI Toolbox for Innovators)

Lecturers can use this persona creator by inputting a research question and specifying their target audience to generate detailed personas that accurately represent their students. This tool helps in understanding the diverse needs, preferences, and behaviors of their audience, allowing for more tailored and effective course design.

For example, a lecturer might enter a research question like, "How do you prefer to engage with course content (e.g., lectures, interactive activities, online modules)?" and specify the target audience as "undergraduate students in a business program." The persona creator would then generate personas that reflect the characteristics and preferences of this group, such as their preferred learning styles and common challenges.

Lecturers can quickly gather valuable insights into their students' needs and preferences, enabling them to create more effective and engaging educational experiences.

Smartcat

for translation purposes

Smartcat is a versatile edtech tool that leverages AI translation, AI content generation, and AI human workflows to streamline translation processes. It supports over 280 languages and 50+ file formats, making it a powerful resource for educational institutions.

Educators can use Smartcat to translate course materials, eLearning content, and other educational resources quickly and accurately. The platform's AI translation technology ensures high-quality translations, while the integrated Marketplace allows educators to collaborate with professional translators and editors. It is stated that this combination of AI and human translation guarantees 100% quality assurance.

Smartcat reduces translation turnaround time, enhances the consistency and scalability of translated content, and simplifies the management of translation projects. This efficiency allows educators to focus more on teaching and less on administrative tasks, ultimately improving the learning experience for students.
