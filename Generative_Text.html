Generative Text

Unlock the power of AI with generative text tools that assist in creating dynamic educational content. These tools can generate essays, reports, summaries, and creative writing prompts, helping students and educators save time and inspire creativity while ensuring high-quality, customized written materials.

ChatGPT

Developed by OpenAI, ChatGPT is one of the most well-known generative text tools. It uses the GPT-4 architecture to generate coherent and contextually relevant text. ChatGPT is widely used for its versatility in creating content, coding assistance, and even casual conversation. Its popularity stems from its accessibility and the continuous improvements made by OpenAI.

Microsoft Copilot

Integrated into many Microsoft products, Copilot uses the same underlying technology as ChatGPT but is tailored for productivity tasks. It assists users in writing documents, generating code, and even managing emails. Copilot’s seamless integration with Microsoft’s ecosystem makes it a favorite among professionals.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Claude (by Anthropic)

Developed by Anthropic, Claude focuses on safety and reliability in AI interactions. It is designed to minimize harmful outputs and provide accurate information, making it a trusted choice for sensitive applications.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Glasp

The Glasp extension is designed to enhance learning efficiency. It allows users to quickly access summaries of YouTube videos and web articles, providing video transcripts in multiple languages and enabling users to highlight and annotate transcripts. This tool supports various AI models like ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude, Mistral AI, and Google Gemini for generating summaries.
Educators can integrate this tool into their courses by using it to provide students with concise summaries of educational videos and articles, making it easier for students to grasp key concepts quickly. They can also use the transcript highlighting feature to emphasize important points and facilitate discussions.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Google Gemini

Google’s Gemini, formerly known as Bard, is another major player in the generative AI space. It is designed to provide timely and updated information, making it particularly useful for tasks that require current data. Gemini’s integration with Google’s services enhances its utility for both personal and professional use.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Maester

MAESTER is a versatile tool designed to generate various types of content, including tutorials, Twitter threads, blog posts, study plans, and more. It also offers features like a grammar checker, code generator, and SQL generator, making it a comprehensive solution for content creation and management.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Perplexity

Known for its ability to handle complex queries and generate detailed responses, Perplexity is popular among users who need in-depth information and analysis.

Go to the tool Price: Free
Rytr

Rytr is a powerful AI writing assistant that generates original and compelling content, making it sound like you, not a robot. 

Go to the tool Price: Free
You.com

You.com is an AI-powered search engine that aims to provide an even better search experience for users. You.com offers a personalized AI chatbot that can assist with various tasks, including writing company-specific emails, drafting SEO content, and elevating your writing. It's like having your own AI co-pilot to enhance productivity.
