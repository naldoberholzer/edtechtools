Academic Phrasebank

The University of Manchester's Academic Phrasebank is a comprehensive resource designed to assist academic writers by providing examples of commonly used phrases organized according to the main sections of a research paper or dissertation. It is particularly useful for non-native English speakers, but native speakers can also benefit from its structured guidance.

Educators can use the Academic Phrasebank to help students improve their academic writing skills by incorporating these phrases into their assignments and research papers. It serves as a valuable teaching tool for demonstrating effective academic language and structure.
Students can use the Phrasebank to enhance their writing by finding appropriate phrases for various sections of their papers, such as introductions, methods, results, and discussions. This can help them articulate their ideas more clearly and professionally.

The Academic Phrasebank overall promotes better academic writing practices. It helps students and educators produce well-structured and coherent academic texts, which can improve the overall quality of research outputs. By offering a reliable reference for academic language, it supports the development of essential writing skills that are crucial for academic success.

Education Resources Information Center (ERIC)

The Education Resources Information Center (ERIC) is a comprehensive online digital library of education research and information. It provides access to a vast collection of journal articles, research reports, conference papers, and other educational materials. ERIC is widely used to support research, teaching, and learning by offering a centralized resource for high-quality academic content.

LibKey

LibKey.io provides instant access to millions of scholarly articles through library subscriptions. It simplifies the process of finding and accessing academic content by allowing users to search for articles using DOIs or PMIDs, ensuring that they can quickly locate the resources they need.

Educators can use LibKey.io to streamline their research process, easily finding and integrating high-quality academic articles into their course materials. Students can use LibKey.io to efficiently gather sources for their assignments, projects, and research papers.

Library Genesis+

Library Genesis+ (LibGen+) is a digital library that provides access to a vast collection of academic articles, books, and other scholarly resources. It offers free access to a wide range of academic materials, which can be particularly beneficial for institutions and individuals with limited access to traditional library resources.

LibGen+ democratizes access to academic knowledge. It helps bridge the gap between resource-rich and resource-poor institutions, ensuring that all students and educators have the opportunity to access high-quality academic materials. This can lead to a more equitable and enriched educational experience, fostering a culture of continuous learning and academic excellence.

Online Learning Journal

The Online Learning Journal (OLJ) is a premier open-access journal published by the Online Learning Consortium (OLC), dedicated to advancing quality digital teaching and learning experiences. 

Educators can use OLJ to stay informed about the latest research and developments in online learning. By accessing articles and studies published in OLJ, they can incorporate evidence-based practices into their teaching, enhance their instructional strategies, and stay updated on emerging trends and technologies. Students can use OLJ to support their academic research, gain insights into effective online learning strategies, and explore topics related to digital education for their projects and assignments.

ResearchGate

ResearchGate is designed to connect researchers and academics worldwide. It allows users to share their publications, ask and answer questions, and find collaborators. Educators can use ResearchGate to integrate current research into their courses, keeping students updated with the latest developments in their field. They can also encourage students to engage with the platform by following researchers, accessing full-text articles, and participating in discussions.

ResearchGate fosters a collaborative learning atmosphere, enhances access to a vast repository of academic resources, and supports the professional development of both educators and students by connecting them with a global network of experts. This can lead to increased research opportunities and a deeper understanding of the subject matter.
