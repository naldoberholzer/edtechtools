Darktable

Open-source photography workflow application and raw developer.

Darktable is an open-source photography workflow application and raw developer, designed for photographers by photographers. It offers a virtual lighttable and darkroom, allowing users to manage digital negatives, view them through a zoomable lighttable, and develop raw images non-destructively. With professional color management and GPU-accelerated processing, Darktable ensures high fidelity and speed in image editing.

Educators can integrate Darktable into photography and digital media courses to teach students about raw image processing, color management, and non-destructive editing techniques. By using Darktable, students can gain hands-on experience with professional-grade tools, enhancing their practical skills and understanding of digital photography workflows.

Darktable offers a cost-effective, powerful tool for teaching advanced photography and image editing. Its open-source nature encourages collaboration and community involvement, fostering a learning environment where students can contribute to and learn from a global community of photographers and developers.

Inkscape

Inkscape is a versatile, open-source vector graphics editor that allows users to create and edit scalable vector graphics (SVG) files. As an edtech tool, it offers a wide range of features such as drawing, shaping, text tools, and path operations, making it ideal for creating detailed and professional-quality graphics.

Educators can integrate Inkscape into their courses to enhance visual learning and creativity.
