Generative Videos

Create engaging and personalized educational videos with these generative tools. They allow users to generate instructional videos, animations, and tutorials quickly, enhancing learning through visual storytelling and dynamic content that can be customized to meet specific educational needs and objectives.

The video looping in the background was created by AI.

BHuman

BHuman is a cutting-edge edtech tool that allows educators to create digital clones that look, sound, and communicate like them. This tool can be used to generate personalized video content for students, making them feel as if the educator recorded the video specifically for them.

Educators can use BHuman to record a short video on the platform using their webcam or phone. This video can then be personalized for each student, providing tailored feedback, instructions, or encouragement. 

Go to the tool Price: Limited free tier Difficulty: Easy
Descript

Descript is an AI-powered, fully featured, end-to-end video editor that simplifies the video creation process. It allows users to cut, copy, and paste video clips, generate words using AI speech, and enhance audio quality. Descript's AI editorial assistant can find clips likely to go viral, translate content quickly, and remove background noise and filler words.

Descript streamlines the video creation process, enhancing the quality of educational content, and making it easier for educators to produce professional-looking videos without needing extensive technical skills. 

Go to the tool Price: $12: 10 transcription hours p/m. Difficulty: Moderate
Hailuo AI

Hailuo AI Video leverages advanced AI technology to simplify and enhance video creation. It automates the video production process, making it easy for users to create high-quality, engaging videos with minimal effort. The tool includes features like automated video trimming, intelligent transitions, and customizable templates, which make it accessible even for those without extensive technical skills.

Educators can use Hailuo AI Video to create dynamic and interactive learning materials. By generating videos that incorporate animations, voiceovers, and high-quality visuals, teachers can present complex concepts in a more engaging and understandable way. Students can also use this tool to create presentations, projects, and other multimedia assignments, allowing them to express their creativity and improve their digital literacy skills.

Go to the tool Price: 100 free credits Difficulty: Easy
Loom

Loom is an AI-powered tool that enhances video communication by allowing users to create and share screen and webcam recordings. Its generative AI video feature automates several aspects of video creation, such as generating titles, summaries, and chapters, as well as removing filler words and silences. This makes the video creation process faster and more efficient.

Educators can use Loom to record and share lessons, provide detailed feedback, and create instructional videos that students can access anytime. The generative AI feature helps streamline these tasks by automatically enhancing videos and generating supplementary materials. Students can use Loom to record presentations, collaborate on projects, and review lectures at their own pace, benefiting from the AI-generated summaries and chapters that make content easier to navigate.

**Loom is also featured as a normal edtech tool used for content creation.

Go to the tool Price: Free starter option Difficulty: Easy
Lumen5

Lumen5 is an AI-powered video creation platform that allows users to easily create engaging video content without any prior training or experience. It transforms written content, such as articles or blog posts, into videos by automatically selecting relevant visuals and text highlights. This tool is particularly useful in education, where it can inspire student interest, support learning with multimedia, and facilitate knowledge construction through video design.

Educators can use Lumen5 to create instructional videos to replace traditional lectures, turn student blog posts into videos for class presentations, or have students create their own videos to explain complex concepts. This not only enhances the learning experience but also encourages creativity and critical thinking among students.

Go to the tool Price: Free & paid options available Difficulty: Easy
RunwayML

One of the exciting tools available on Runway is Gen-3 Alpha, the newest video model. It allows users to create videos from text prompts.

Educators can teach students how to use Gen-3 Alpha to transform written prompts into engaging videos. 

Go to the tool Price: Limited free tier Difficulty: Moderate
Veo (from Google)

Veo is a video generation model that produces high-quality, 1080p resolution videos in various cinematic and visual styles. It accurately captures the nuance and tone of prompts, making it an excellent tool for educators looking to enhance their courses with engaging visual content.

Educators can use Veo to create dynamic educational videos that illustrate complex concepts, provide visual storytelling, and offer immersive learning experiences. For example, a history teacher could generate time-lapse videos of historical events, while a science instructor could create detailed animations of biological processes. The ability to edit and customize videos with specific prompts allows for tailored content that meets the unique needs of each course.

Go to the tool Price: Google account Difficulty: Easy
Vyond

Vyond is an all-in-one AI video creation platform that simplifies the process of making engaging videos. It includes Vyond Go, an AI-powered instant video maker, and Vyond Studio, a full-featured video editor.

Educators can use Vyond to create compelling educational videos quickly and easily. With features like character creation from photos, automatic translation into 70+ languages, and background removal, educators can tailor content to diverse student needs. Vyond can also enhance learning by making complex concepts more accessible and engaging through visual storytelling, ultimately improving student comprehension and retention.

Go to the tool Price: Free trial Difficulty: Easy
