Learning Design Tools

Design impactful and effective learning experiences with a suite of learning design tools.
Optimize your teaching strategies to ensure students remain engaged and achieve learning goals.

Learning Designer

The Learning Designer tool, part of the "Blended and Online Learning Design" course by UCL on FutureLearn, helps educators design and analyze learning activities. Educators can specify activity types, duration, group size, teacher presence, and resources. The tool tracks designed time and provides visual analysis through pie and bar charts, aiding in the review and revision of designs. Educators can categorize, edit, and share their designs, making it easy to collaborate and receive feedback.

In higher education, this tool benefits educators by offering a structured approach to designing diverse and engaging learning experiences. It supports the creation of comprehensive lesson plans that cater to various learning styles, enhancing student engagement and learning outcomes.

Newsela

Newsela provides a vast collection of current event articles, each available at five different reading levels. This platform aims to enhance literacy skills by offering real-world content that is both engaging and educational. Newsela's articles come with questions and writing prompts aligned with common core standards, making it a versatile resource for educators.

Educators can integrate Newsela into their courses by using it to supplement their curriculum with relevant, up-to-date articles that match their students' reading abilities. Teachers can assign articles as reading assignments, use them to spark classroom discussions, or incorporate them into writing exercises. The platform's integration with tools like Google Classroom and Microsoft Teams makes it easy to assign and track student progress.

In the higher education environment, Newsela provides students with access to diverse perspectives on current events, fostering critical thinking, and improving reading comprehension. The ability to adjust reading levels ensures that all students, regardless of their proficiency, can engage with the material meaningfully, promoting inclusivity and a deeper understanding of the subject matter.

Read-o-Meter

The Read-o-Meter is a tool designed to estimate reading time based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. Educators can use this tool to help students manage their reading assignments more effectively by providing accurate estimates of how long it will take to read specific texts. This can be particularly useful in planning lessons and ensuring that students allocate sufficient time for their reading tasks.

Read-o-Meter can enhance time management skills, allowing students to better plan their study schedules. It also helps educators design more balanced coursework by aligning reading assignments with realistic time expectations, ultimately improving student engagement and learning outcomes.

TED-ed

TED-Ed is designed to spark curiosity and enhance learning through engaging video-based lessons. Educators can create lessons around TED-Ed Animations, TED Talks, or YouTube videos, adding interactive questions and discussion topics to deepen understanding. These lessons can be shared online, allowing teachers to track student progress and engagement.

TED-Ed provides a dynamic way to present complex ideas, encourages critical thinking, and fosters a global perspective by exposing students to diverse viewpoints. Additionally, the platform's professional development programs, like the TED Masterclass, help educators refine their presentation skills and create impactful TED-style talks, further enriching the learning experience.

ReadSpeaker

ReadSpeaker is a tool that provides text-to-speech (TTS) solutions, allowing users to listen to written content in natural-sounding voices. It supports a wide range of content types, including courses, PDFs, eBooks, web pages, and more, making it accessible on various devices such as smartphones, computers, and tablets.

ReadSpeaker can benefit both educators and students by improving their learning and teaching experiences. Students can listen to their course materials, which can help with comprehension and retention, particularly for those who have learning disabilities or language barriers. ReadSpeaker enables educators to create audio versions of their content, making it more accessible and engaging for all students.

ReadSpeaker promotes inclusivity and accessibility in higher education, which has significant benefits. It enables students with diverse learning needs to better access and comprehend course materials, resulting in improved academic performance. It also encourages compliance with accessibility standards, which ensure that educational institutions provide a high-quality, inclusive learning environment for all students.
