Productivity / Academic Performance

Bionic Reading

Bionic Reading is a reading enhancement tool that combines traditional reading with technology-assisted techniques to help users read faster, improve comprehension, and retain more information. It uses bolding of the first half of each word to allow the brain to process text more efficiently. Additionally, it can alter images and background colors to reduce eye strain.

Educators can use Bionic Reading in both physical and online classrooms to enhance students' reading efficiency. In a physical classroom, teachers can provide printed materials with bolded text to help students read faster and retain more information. Online, educators can use browser extensions or apps to apply Bionic Reading techniques to digital texts, making it easier for students to process and understand course materials.

In higher education, Bionic Reading can significantly benefit students by improving their reading speed and comprehension, allowing them to cover more material in less time. This can lead to better retention of information, reduced cognitive load, and increased productivity, ultimately enhancing their academic performance.

Calendly

Calendly is a versatile meeting scheduling app that allows users to set up timeslots and share them via a link. Individuals or groups can then sign up for the most convenient time. You can collect essential information such as email addresses, phone numbers, and meeting locations. As timeslots are booked, they disappear, preventing double bookings. Rescheduling is managed by the person who signed up, reducing your workload. Additionally, Calendly automatically adjusts available times to the viewer's time zone, eliminating the need to coordinate different time zones. This feature is particularly useful for online meetings, Skype sessions, or phone calls with distant participants.

Calendly is an excellent tool in higher education for educators who need students to schedule meetings or conferences. It integrates seamlessly with other programs like Slack and Google Calendar, automatically populating events and preventing scheduling conflicts.

Monday.com

Monday.com is a versatile work management platform that can be effectively utilized as an edtech tool. It offers a range of features such as project management, task organization, and collaboration tools, making it ideal for educational settings.

Educators can use Monday.com to manage course planning, track assignments, and facilitate group projects. The platform's customizable templates and integrations with other tools streamline the workflow, allowing teachers to focus more on instruction and less on administrative tasks. In higher education, Monday.com enhances student engagement and collaboration, supports diverse teaching methods, and provides a flexible environment for both in-person and remote learning.

 Time Tracking Power-Up (by Everhour)

The Time Tracking Power-Up by Everhour is a tool designed to integrate seamlessly with Trello, allowing users to track time, set estimates, and generate detailed reports directly within their project management boards. This tool is particularly useful for managing and monitoring the time spent on various tasks and projects, making it an excellent resource for both educators and students.

Educators can use the Time Tracking Power-Up to manage their course-related tasks more efficiently. By tracking the time spent on preparing lectures, grading assignments, and conducting research, they can optimize their workflow and ensure they are allocating their time effectively. Students can benefit from this tool by tracking the time they spend on different study activities, helping them to manage their schedules better and stay on top of their coursework.

This tool promotes better time management and productivity by helping both educators and students stay organized and focused, leading to improved academic performance and a more structured approach to learning and teaching. Additionally, the detailed reports generated by the tool offer valuable insights into how time is being utilized, enabling users to make informed decisions about their time management strategies.
