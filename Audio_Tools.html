Audio Tools

Ardour

Ardour is a powerful digital audio workstation (DAW) that offers extensive features for recording, editing, mixing, and mastering audio and MIDI projects. It supports a wide range of audio formats and plugins, making it a versatile tool for various audio production needs. With its open-source nature, Ardour provides transparency and flexibility, allowing users to customize and extend its functionality.

Students can gain hands-on experience with professional-grade audio tools, learning how to record and edit audio, create soundtracks for video projects, and experiment with different sound effects and instruments. The software's comprehensive feature set and user-friendly interface make it accessible for both beginners and advanced users.

Go to the tool Price: Free (open source) Difficulty: Moderate
Audacity

Audacity is a powerful, free, and open-source software for recording and editing audio, making it an excellent edtech tool. It supports all major audio formats and runs on Windows, MacOS, and Linux. With features like versioned backups, cloud saving, and a vast selection of third-party plugins, Audacity allows educators to create high-quality audio content for their courses.

Educators can use Audacity to record lectures, create podcasts, and produce voice-over content for presentations. The software's ability to visualize frequencies and use scientific analyzers can also be beneficial for teaching subjects like music, linguistics, and audio engineering.

Go to the tool Price: Free Difficulty: Moderate
Soundtrap

Soundtrap for Education empowers students and teachers to explore creative sound recording across various subjects and for all ages and ability levels. It offers features like interactive transcripts, high-quality audio collaboration, and a vast library of beats and presets. Educators can use Soundtrap to enhance presentations, create collaborative projects, and record podcasts. The platform integrates seamlessly with major LMS systems, making it easy to manage assignments and lesson plans.

Go to the tool Price: 30-day free trial for educators Difficulty: Moderate
Spotify for Podcasters (formerly Anchor.fm)

Spotify for Podcasters is a comprehensive platform that allows users to create, distribute, and monetize podcasts. It offers tools for recording, editing, and publishing audio content, making it accessible for educators to produce high-quality educational podcasts.
Educators can use Spotify for Podcasters in their courses to create engaging audio content, such as lecture recordings, interviews with experts, and supplementary materials. This can enhance the learning experience by providing students with flexible, on-the-go access to course content.
